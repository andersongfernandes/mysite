WhatsPauta

This product is designed for content creators such as news portals, television, and radio stations, with the aim of managing story ideas that come in via WhatsApp.

The solution manages incoming messages, making the content available to multiple users simultaneously, while categorizing everything by editorial topics and the format of the sent item (text, video, image, audio, or file).

Furthermore, it offers a management module with relevant metrics for managers to track communication performance with their audience.

Finally, it allows content producers to extract insights from messages through Artificial Intelligence (AI)-powered surveys.


Technologies used: javascript, node.js, php, chatGPT, MySQL, HTML, CSS and chart.js
